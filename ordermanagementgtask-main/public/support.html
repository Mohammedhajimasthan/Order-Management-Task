Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the Orders page and click the "Add Order" button in the top-right. Fill in the customer, product, amount, and status details, then save.
On the Orders page, apply any filters you want (the export will respect your current filter). Then click "CSV" or "Excel" to download the data.
Go to Settings and scroll to the "Business Logo" section. Click "Choose File", select your image (PNG, JPG, SVG up to 5MB), and it will upload automatically to Firebase Storage.
Go to Settings → Notifications section and click "Enable Push Notifications". Your browser will ask for permission. Once granted, you'll receive notifications for new orders and ticket updates.
Click the moon/sun icon in the top navigation bar on any page, or go to Settings and toggle "Dark Mode". Your preference is saved locally.
